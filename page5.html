Transfer Information

You can either reach this Island by a Chartered Private Speedboat, or by Public methods of Transfer

Chartered Private Speedboat

The cost of Chartered Speedboat is USD 100.00 per way for 01 to 05 persons. And it costs USD 170.00 per way for 06 to 15 persons. You must request a day before your arrival to make arrangements. We would require a copy of the Flight tickets of all guests for verification purpose. We will be there at Airport with our Sign board to Welcome you. Total duration is 20 minutes. 

Public Methods of Transfer

Below are the Public methods of Transfer. You have to Travel to the capital city Male', to travel with these methods of Transfer. Advanced booking is required for Speedboat 1 and Speedboat 2. Contact us for bookings. 


How to reach Male' city:

- When you arrive at the Airport, go to the Information counter and ask for directions to the Airport Ferry to Male'. After locating the Ferry, buy the ticket for $1 per person and travel to Male'. It will take less than 10 minutes to reach Male'. Or you can get on a Taxi and Travel to any where at Male'. Taxi charge is $7 - $12
